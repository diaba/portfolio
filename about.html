About me


I always had a passion for the field of STEM(Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) and I believe that I can do something to make difference in the world. I grew up in a country where Girls were provided with less participation opportunities as compared to their male counterparts. It is more severe in STEM higher education. Along the readblocks, I studied Mathematic and Physics Applied to Computer Science. With a Master’s degree in Computer Science, I worked as an IT assistant first. Then, I taught programing java and web development for a private professional school.

I joined my husband to us in 2011. It took me 2 years to learn English and settle down. I a mom of 2 lovely boys. During the pandemic, I had my moment to clarity and realized that I wouldn’t let anything stop me pursuing my passion in Computer Science, I began to chase that dream that leads me where I am today. I enrolled in as many courses as I could find and built more skill sets in Java, Python, Flask, Django, Vb, English composition, UX design and javascript. But I didn’t stop there.

I believe that with a right education and knowledge anyone can reach a full potential and make dream a reality. So, I am in Google UX Design certification training and I participate at Interact java Immerse training. In my free time , I give tutoring session to students in math and computer science, give back to community and keep my mind and body moving. I love drinking tea called ataya. I enjoy learning new thing, growing and finding new opportunities. If you have questions or want to work with me. Please don’t hesitate to reach out, I am always happy to help.
